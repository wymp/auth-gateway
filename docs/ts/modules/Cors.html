    Get a CORS Handler

    Handles details of the CORS protocol - including the preflight OPTIONS request - on a per-request basis. By default, allows GET requests with auth, content type and accept headers.

    NOTE: Because this library was designed to perform authn/z internally, it mostly just "turns off" CORS functionality. This may or may not be desirable to you in your particular use case.**
